Our Services

Carpal is a vehicle maintenance assistant app designed to help everyday car owners perform basic repairs and upkeep themselves; saving time and money. Many simple tasks, like changing a light bulb or tire, seem intimidating without guidance. Our goal is to empower users by walking them through these tasks step-by-step.

About CarPal

CarPal was built by a student team at the University of Nebraska-Omaha who share a passion for making car care more accessible. Too often, simple repairs and maintenance tasks feel overwhelming without guidance, so we created CarPal to empower drivers to take control of their vehicle’s health.

Our Team

We’re computer science students, car enthusiasts, and problem-solvers who believe in practical technology. Each member brought unique skills - from coding and UX design to hands-on auto knowledge - to make CarPal simple, effective, and user-friendly.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce the stress of car ownership by giving everyday drivers the confidence to perform DIY repairs and maintenance. With step-by-step instructions, CarPal saves you money, time, and the headache of unnecessary trips to the shop.
